keyframes & animations

Keyframes are used to animate the properties of an element. Animations are used to animate the properties of an element.

Properties of Keyframes

  • animation-name: The name of the keyframes animation.
  • animation-duration: The duration of the animation.
  • animation-iteration-count: The number of times the animation will run.
  • animation-direction: The direction of the animation.
  • animation-timing-function: The timing function of the animation.
  • animation-delay: The delay of the animation.
